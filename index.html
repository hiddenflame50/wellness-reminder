Privacy Policy for Wellness Reminder Extension

Last Updated:

Quick Summary: Wellness Reminder is a privacy-focused Chrome extension that stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally

The Wellness Reminder extension stores the following information locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Reminder Settings: Your preferred intervals for break, stretching, and mindfulness reminders
  • Progress Data: Exercise completion counts, daily progress, and streak information
  • Achievement Data: Unlocked achievements and milestone progress
  • Preference Settings: Your customization preferences for notifications and display

1.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Browsing history or website data
  • Location data
  • Any data from other applications or extensions
  • Analytics or usage tracking data

2. How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Functionality: To provide reminder notifications at your specified intervals
  • Progress Tracking: To track your wellness exercise completion and maintain streaks
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences and settings
  • Achievement System: To unlock and display your wellness achievements

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your data
  • Your data is encrypted by Chrome's built-in security
  • Only you have access to your wellness data

3.2 Data Backup

If you have Chrome sync enabled, your extension data may be synchronized across your Chrome browsers on different devices. This is handled entirely by Google Chrome and is subject to Google's privacy policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your data to any third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, there is no data to share.

5. Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions for legitimate functionality:

  • Storage: To save your preferences and progress locally
  • Notifications: To send wellness reminders and achievement notifications
  • Alarms: To schedule reminders at your specified intervals
  • Active Tab: To display wellness exercises and breathing guides on your current page

6. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View Data: All your data is visible within the extension popup
  • Modify Data: You can change settings and reset progress at any time
  • Delete Data: Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data
  • Export Data: You can manually record your progress if desired

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or adults. The wellness content is appropriate for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs. It operates entirely within Chrome's ecosystem.

10. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device until you:

  • Manually reset your progress within the extension
  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear your Chrome extension data
